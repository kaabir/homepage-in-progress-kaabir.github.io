

## Personal Academic Website for PhD Student/Postdoc

A modern, professional academic website showcasing your research, publications, and professional profile, designed to be easily hosted on GitHub Pages.

---

### 🏠 Homepage / About Me
A clean, welcoming landing page featuring:
- Professional photo placeholder
- Brief introduction and research interests
- Your current position and institution affiliation
- Quick links to key sections (publications, research, CV)
- Social/academic profile links (Google Scholar, ORCID, Twitter/X, GitHub)

---

### 📚 Publications Page
A dedicated page to showcase your academic work:
- List of publications organized by year (newest first)
- Each entry shows: title, authors, venue/journal, year
- Links to paper PDFs, DOIs, or preprint servers
- Filter/search functionality to easily find specific papers
- Citation count display option

---

### 🔬 Research / Projects
Highlight your research areas and ongoing projects:
- Visual cards for each research area or project
- Project descriptions with key details
- Images or diagrams where relevant
- Links to related publications or resources
- Collaborator acknowledgments

---

### 📄 CV / Resume
Easy access to your curriculum vitae:
- Embedded PDF viewer or downloadable PDF link
- Option for a web-based version highlighting key accomplishments
- Education, experience, awards, and skills sections

---

### 📝 Blog / News Section
Keep visitors updated on your academic journey:
- List of blog posts or news items with date and preview
- Individual post pages with full content
- Categories or tags for organization
- Note: This will be static content (no backend database), with posts stored as data files in your project

---

### ✉️ Contact Page
Make it easy for collaborators and opportunities to reach you:
- Contact form with name, email, and message fields
- Form submissions sent via a third-party service (like Formspree or EmailJS - no backend needed)
- Your office address and institutional email
- Links to social/academic profiles

---

### 🎨 Design & User Experience
- **Modern & Professional**: Clean typography, subtle animations, polished UI components
- **Responsive**: Works beautifully on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Dark/Light mode**: Toggle for reader preference
- **Navigation**: Fixed header with easy access to all sections
- **Consistent branding**: Cohesive color scheme throughout

---

### 🚀 GitHub Pages Ready
- Static site with no backend required
- All content easily editable in code
- Optimized for fast loading
- Instructions for deploying to GitHub Pages
